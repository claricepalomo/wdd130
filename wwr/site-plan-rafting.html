Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this activity is to navigate downstream rivers rapids using an inflated raft. It's considered an extreme sport which is highly challenging and risky and requires a lot of technical skills and teamwork.It also helps strengthens your body, mind and emotional ability.

Audience

Target Audience

The white water rafting website targets adventure enthusiasts aged 18-45, both genders, with middle to upper-middle-class incomes. They seek adrenaline rushes, value outdoor experiences, and rely on mobile devices and social media for trip planning
